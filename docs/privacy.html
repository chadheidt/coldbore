Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Loadscope (the "app") is a macOS desktop application sold and operated by Chad Heidt ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information is and is not collected. Loadscope is built to be local-first: your load data never leaves your computer.

The app

  • No account. Loadscope has no sign-up, login, or user account.
  • Your data stays on your Mac. The app reads the CSV files you provide and writes to a standard Excel workbook stored locally on your computer. We never receive, transmit, or store your load data, chronograph data, or shooting data.
  • No telemetry or analytics. The app does not track usage or send analytics.
  • Update checks. To offer one-click updates, the app periodically requests a small public version file over the internet. This request contains no personal data beyond the standard network information (such as an IP address) inherent to any web request.
  • License validation. If you purchase a license, the app may verify the license key you enter against our license server. Only the license key (and a non-identifying check) is sent; no personal data or load data is transmitted.

The website (loadscope.app)

  • Beta access requests. If you request beta access, you submit your name and email so we can review the request and email you an access code. We use this only to contact you about access to Loadscope.
  • Bot protection. The access-request form uses Cloudflare Turnstile to block automated abuse. Cloudflare processes this under its own privacy terms.
  • No tracking cookies or ad pixels. The site sets no advertising or analytics cookies.

Purchases

Purchases are processed by Lemon Squeezy, which acts as the Merchant of Record. Payment information (such as card details) is collected and handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy under their privacy policy and terms; we do not see or store your payment card information. We receive only the order and license information needed to deliver your license key and provide support.

Data requests

Because we hold almost no personal data (essentially only beta-access request emails and order records via Lemon Squeezy), requests are simple. To ask what we have, or to have it deleted, email support@loadscope.app.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. The "last updated" date above will change accordingly.

Questions: support@loadscope.app
